--- title: Survey, Develop personas for accessibility guidance ---

Welcome to this survey, and thank you for agreeing to participate!

This is an informal survey and the intent is to gather data to better understand our target audience for our website Digital Accessibility Toolkit / Sharing space.

The collected data will be used to expand our personas; including their goal, frustration and use case.

Please note, that some questions will be required and some will be optional. In either case it will be identified at the end of the question.

The activity should take a maximum of 10 to 15 minutes to complete.
